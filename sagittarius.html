♐ Sagittarius

Sagittarius individuals are adventurous, optimistic, and philosophical. They love travel and freedom. Freedom is your fuel. You’re adventurous, optimistic, and allergic to anything that smells like routine. Career: Travel, teaching, journalism, or startups — anything with a mission and movement. Sitting still isn’t an option. Behavior: Honest to a fault, always learning, and sometimes unintentionally blunt. You're like a walking TED Talk with zero filter. Future: Wild and unpredictable, but full of growth and experience. You’ll probably change careers (and hairstyles) at least 7 times. Love Life: You need a partner in adventure — not someone who’ll chain you down. Romance is fun, as long as it doesn’t feel like homework. You booked a one-way flight to “finding yourself” and never came back. Chill, Sag — not everything is a gap year.
