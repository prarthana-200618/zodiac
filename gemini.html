♊ Gemini

You are a curious soul with a brain that never hits pause. One minute you’re into philosophy, the next you’re rearranging your closet by color and genre of emotion. Career: You shine in communication-heavy roles — media, sales, teaching, or anything that lets you talk, think, and switch gears often. Sitting still is a crime in your world. Behavior: Adaptable and witty, you're the life of every party and the ghost of every group chat. You multitask like it’s a sport. Future: It’s bright — assuming you can finish what you start. Your ideas are golden, but your attention span? Hmm... Love Life: Flirty and fun, you’re charming as hell. Commitment? Well... depends on how interesting they are next week. You change your mind more than people change their Netflix passwords. Calm down, Gemini — not everything needs a plot twist.
