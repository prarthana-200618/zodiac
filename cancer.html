♋ Cancer

Soft on the outside, softer on the inside — you’re an emotional powerhouse who feels everything deeply. You’re intuitive, protective, and seriously sentimental. Career: Nursing, counseling, education, home decor — anything where your nurturing nature can shine. You’re the ultimate caretaker. Behavior: Quietly observant, you absorb the room’s energy before deciding whether to stay or run. You love hard, but you’re also hard to get to know. Future: Emotional growth will be your superpower. As you learn to trust more, you’ll build something that lasts — both in love and in career. Love Life: You love love. Deeply, honestly, and intensely. Just don’t expect you to move on easily — you’re emotionally Velcro. You cried during a cereal commercial once, didn’t you? It’s okay, Cancer — we all need a human tissue box sometimes.
