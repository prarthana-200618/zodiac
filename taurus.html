♉ Taurus

You are grounded, patient, and loyal — the true rock among the zodiac signs. Stability and comfort are your life goals, and you don’t rush into anything without being sure. Career: You’re a natural in finance, design, architecture, and anything that allows for patience and precision. You work hard, and you love seeing tangible results. Behavior: Calm and composed — until someone moves your favorite mug or questions your routine. You’re not stubborn, just... committed (okay, fine, you're stubborn). Future: Slow and steady truly wins your race. You’ll build a secure, comfortable life brick by brick, and you’ll protect it with your life. Love Life: You’re deeply romantic and committed once you choose someone — but that person better love cuddles, good food, and not touching the thermostat. You’d win “Most Likely to Fight Someone for Stealing Your Favorite Spot on the Couch.” Move much, Taurus?
