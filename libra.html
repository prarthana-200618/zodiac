♎ Libra

Libras are fair, diplomatic, and social. They value harmony, beauty, and strong relationships. You're graceful, charming, and allergic to conflict. If diplomacy were a sport, you’d have more gold medals than Michael Phelps. Career: Law, fashion, counseling, or design — anywhere beauty and balance matter. You work best in partnerships, whether romantic or business. Behavior: You weigh everything, literally. Choosing a sandwich feels like writing a peace treaty. But your kindness makes people feel seen. Future: Smooth sailing — if you can stop being so indecisive. Your talent lies in connecting people and making peace, just remember to choose your own side too. Love Life: You’re all about romance, harmony, and emotional balance. You fall in love with love, and you hate being alone more than you hate confrontation. You take longer to choose a Netflix show than it took to end the Cold War. Pick a side, Libra — even if it’s just chocolate vs. vanilla.
